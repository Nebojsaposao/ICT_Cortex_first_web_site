How to brew the perfect cup

  • Start with fresh, high-quality coffee beans.
  • Grind the beans just before brewing for maximum freshness.
  • Use clean, filtered water, and ensure the right water temperature (around 195–205°F).
  • Measure the coffee and water ratio correctly, typically 1:15 (coffee:water).
  • Brew using your preferred method (drip, French press, pour-over, etc.) for the ideal extraction time.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your perfect cup of coffee!
